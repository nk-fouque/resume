What's a double degree ?

A double degree is an agreement that lets you pursue two diplomas at the same time with each school acknowledging the other's classes as equivalent to some of theirs and by completing the right modules you qualify for both diplomas.
Having undertaken a university's double degree as an Engineering student, I learned about the world of research without compromising on a heavy technical background.
